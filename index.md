# AI Learning Center

Welcome to the AI Learning Center.

Artificial Intelligence is rapidly transforming the legal profession, business, medicine, education, software development, and countless other fields. Yet for many people, AI remains a mysterious black box.

This learning center was created to make AI understandable for everyone — especially students, professionals, and lifelong learners who do not have a technical background.

The goal is simple:

* Understand how AI began
* Learn how modern AI systems work
* Explore the technologies behind ChatGPT and other AI tools
* Understand the opportunities and limitations of AI
* Prepare for a future where humans and AI increasingly work together
