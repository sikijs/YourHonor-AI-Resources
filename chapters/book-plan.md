# AI Learning Center Book Plan

## Project Overview

Title:

**The Story of Artificial Intelligence: A Beginner's Guide to Understanding AI**

Audience:

- Law students
- Legal professionals
- Business professionals
- Curious non-technical readers
- Anyone who wants to understand AI without a computer science background

Writing Style:

- Conversational and friendly
- Explain technical concepts using plain English
- Avoid unnecessary jargon
- Use stories, analogies, and real-world examples
- Every chapter should feel approachable and enjoyable
- Assume the reader has no technical background

Visual Philosophy:

Many AI concepts are invisible.

Wherever possible, use:

- Simple diagrams
- Concept illustrations
- Timelines
- Comparison graphics
- Flowcharts

The goal is understanding, not technical precision.
